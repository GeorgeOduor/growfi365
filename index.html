Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common questions.

Do you hold or manage my savings?

No. We never handle your money. You save in your own bank account, M-PESA, or wherever you choose. The KSh 100 is a one-time commitment fee to join the cohort, not a savings deposit.

Is this financial advice or investment guidance?

No. We are not financial advisors. GrowFi365 is solely about building a savings habit through accountability and discipline. We don't tell you where or how to save, just help you build the consistency.

What happens if I miss check-ins?

If you miss a weekly check-in without prior notice, the group will call you out. Repeated misses may result in removal from the group without a refund. This strict policy keeps everyone accountable and ensures serious participants only.

Is the KSh 100 fee refundable?

No. The fee is non-refundable to ensure commitment from all participants. This isn't a money-back guarantee program—it's a commitment device. The fee is small enough to be accessible but meaningful enough to ensure seriousness.
